About t{h}inker.

T{h}inker is an Android app that provides an outlet for students suffering ADD/ADHD and anxiety through color stimulation and (optional) calming vibrations through their phones. This app can be used during lesson without being a distraction for students and teachers.

Devised by a group of girls in a library’s computer lab, T{h}inker was a product of intense brainstorming over pizza and unexpected friendships. Beginning to focus on the technology available in today’s classrooms and everyday life, an idea of an app based on the ‘calm’ part of life came to be.

Perfect for anxiety, ADD/ADHD, or when you need a moment of ‘calm’ in the midst of the day’s many focuses, T{h}inker provides an outlet for many people through color simulation and calming vibrations through their phones.
